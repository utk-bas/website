Responsibilities:

The President is responsible with working with the entire team and faculty advisor to put on engaging events with companies and opportunties for students. The president will also serve on the Haslam Presidents' Council.

The VP of Marketing is responsible for running our social media pages, like Facebook and Instagram. In past years, VP of Marketing has worked to design t-shirts for the society.

The VP of Operations is responsible for working with the faculty advisor to book meeting rooms and coordinate any food at meetings.

The VP of Finance is responsible of keeping track of dues and managing the society's finances.
